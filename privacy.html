Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: February 2026  |  Effective Date: February 2026

1. Introduction

My Automation Hub ("we", "our", or "I") is a solo automation consultancy operated by Saudagar Zeeshan. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use my website or services.

By using this website or engaging my services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. I am committed to handling your data responsibly and transparently.

2. Information I Collect

I only collect information that is necessary to provide my services or respond to your enquiries. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and phone number provided via the booking form or direct contact
  • Business Information: Company name, industry, and automation requirements you share during consultation
  • Booking Data: Meeting date, time, and preferences collected through Calendly
  • Usage Data: Basic website analytics such as pages visited and time spent (collected via tools like Google Analytics, if enabled)
  • Communication Records: Emails and messages exchanged during the project

⚠️ I do not collect payment card numbers directly. All payments are processed through secure third-party payment providers.

3. How I Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for legitimate business purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and schedule consultations
  • To scope, build, and deliver automation projects
  • To send project updates and communicate progress
  • To provide post-delivery support and follow-up
  • To improve my services based on feedback
  • To comply with legal obligations

I will never sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Tools

To deliver my services, I use a limited number of trusted third-party tools. These tools may process your data as part of normal operation:

  • Calendly — for scheduling consultations (subject to Calendly's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Workspace — for email, meetings, and file sharing
  • n8n / Zapier / Make.com — used to build your automation workflows
  • GitHub Pages — for hosting this website

I only share the minimum amount of information necessary with these tools and choose providers that maintain strong privacy standards.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in password-protected systems. I take the following measures to protect your information:

  • Access to client data is restricted to me only
  • Project files are stored with appropriate access controls
  • Sensitive credentials shared during projects are not retained after delivery
  • All tools used comply with industry-standard security practices

While no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, I take all reasonable precautions to protect your personal information.

6. Data Retention

I retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Enquiry and consultation data: retained for up to 12 months
  • Active project data: retained for the duration of the project plus 6 months
  • Financial records: retained for up to 7 years as required by law
  • Communication records: retained for up to 2 years for reference

You may request deletion of your data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the data I hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Ask me to limit how I use your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent to marketing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me directly via the booking form or email.

8. Cookies

This website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. These are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser. No tracking cookies or advertising cookies are used on this site.

9. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When I do, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of this website after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please get in touch:

  • Via the booking/contact form on this website
  • Through my Calendly scheduling page

I aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 48 hours.
