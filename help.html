Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is business automation?

+
Business automation means using software to perform repetitive tasks automatically ‚Äî without you having to do them manually each time. For example: automatically sending a welcome email when someone fills a form, updating a spreadsheet when an order is placed, or routing support tickets to the right person. It saves time, reduces errors, and lets you focus on growing your business.

How does the process work ‚Äî from consultation to delivery?

+
Step 1: You book a free 30-minute consultation call. Step 2: We discuss your business, your pain points, and what you'd like to automate. Step 3: I send you a clear project scope and price. Step 4: Once agreed, I build and test the automation. Step 5: I walk you through the final solution and hand it over. You're involved at every stage and kept informed throughout.

What tools and platforms do you work with?

+
I work with a wide range of automation platforms including n8n, Zapier, Make.com (Integromat), and custom API integrations. For business tools, I can connect Google Workspace (Gmail, Sheets, Calendar, Drive), Slack, Notion, Airtable, HubSpot, Calendly, Stripe, WhatsApp Business API, and hundreds of other applications.

How long does a typical project take?

+
Most automation projects are completed within 3 to 7 business days after the project scope is agreed. Simpler single-workflow automations can be delivered faster. More complex systems with multiple integrations may take longer. I always give you a realistic timeline upfront ‚Äî and I stick to it.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use the automations you build?

+
Not at all. I build automations that run in the background without any technical input from you. When I hand over the project, I walk you through exactly how it works in plain language. I also provide documentation so you can reference it later. If something needs adjusting, just reach out and I'll handle it.

Will you need access to my accounts or data?

+
For most integrations, I'll need read/write access to specific tools ‚Äî for example, connecting your Gmail or Google Sheets to an automation. I request only the minimum permissions needed to build the workflow. All credentials are handled securely and never shared. After delivery, you can revoke any access you granted during the build process.

What if the automation breaks or stops working after delivery?

+
Every project includes a 7-day post-delivery support window where I'll fix any bugs or issues at no extra charge. If a third-party platform makes changes that affect your automation after that window, I'll assess and fix it at a reasonable rate. I'm always reachable and won't leave you stranded.

How much does automation cost?

+
Pricing depends on the complexity of the automation, the number of tools being connected, and the time required to build and test it. A simple single-workflow automation starts from an affordable flat fee. More complex multi-step systems are priced transparently after scoping. The free consultation call is the best way to get an accurate quote for your specific needs.
